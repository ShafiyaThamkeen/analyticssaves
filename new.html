About Us


The vision of Analytics Saves at Work (aSaw) is to be a leading analytics consulting firm known for value creation for its clients through innovation. The values guiding aSaw in realising the vision are innovation, excellence, integrity and collaboration which together define the culture of the company. With an in-house team of data scientists, software developers, consultants and industry experts, we ensure that the data works for our clients and support data-driven decision making for competitive advantage. Our mission is to help firms unlock values of data and discover hidden efficiencies to reduce costs and improve revenues.
